Elizabethtown Podcast is a new podcast dedicated to showing off the incredible people in the growing city of Elizabethtown, KY. Each week, we highlight stories of neighbors, local business owners and community leaders. Subscribe to hear a new story each week about Kentucky's fastest growing city.
